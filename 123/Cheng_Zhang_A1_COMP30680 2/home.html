FAQ'S

Every stage is worth leaving a beautiful moment.

Q1 What documents are needed to rent a car?

ID card. driver license. Foreign customers provide passports that are valid for more than two months beyond the current lease term.

Q2 What invoices can be issued?

After the domestic rental order is returned and settled, the value-added tax invoice will be issued for you based on the order unit; electronic invoices and paper invoices are supported.

Q3 What online pre-order methods are used?

APP: Download the UCD Car Rental App through the App Market to make a reservation.
Browser: Enter ucdcarrental.com reservation in the browser

Q4 What are the rental fees?

Compulsory fees include: vehicle rental and service fees, basic service fees, and vehicle maintenance fees.
Other costs include: delivery service fee, door-to-door pickup service fee, service fee for returning the car from another place, refueling service fee, overtime service fee, etc.
